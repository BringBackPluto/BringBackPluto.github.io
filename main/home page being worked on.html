We take user privacy seriously

Designed to assist people, Lifelong collects minimal user data in order to give you useful suggestions and recommendations. These recommendations vary from small activity suggestions such as moutain hiking to suggesting possible future career paths such as giving you a suggestion to try out computer programing. Lifelong's suggestions are tailored specifically to you because of the minimal data it collects from you. All the information stored by Lifelong is very secure. This information is solely used to better Lifelong's suggestions and will never be shared to anyone. If you would like to know more regarding what data we collect and how we store it, please visit our privacy policy page.
