Privacy

Lifelong Privacy Policy

By using our application, you've put faith in us to keep your personal information safe. We acknowledge that this is a great responsibility and we strive to improve every day. You always have control over what information you share and what we are allowed to do with it.

The types of information we collect as you use our application

The advantages of our application are best demonstrated when we can optimize our services depending on the preferences of our users. This is achieved through the collection of various types of information, ranging from superficial data such as the language you speak and your age, to more personalized data such as which region you reside, your personality traits or what activities you might find interesting.


The types of activity we may collect include(at your discretion, as always):

  • Your location data
  • Activity preferences
  • Interactions with content
  • Answers to surveys and questionnaires
  • Your interactions with family and friends
  • The degrees of success of recommendations
  • Activity on third-party sites and apps that share our services


Maintaining and improving our services

Your information is also used to ensure our application is working as intended, such as tracking inconsistencies in our data analysis or troubleshooting issues that you report to us. Providing personalized services

What is done with all this data you may ask? Well, the most immediate result is to customize what our application brings you. For example, we will provide you with the information in regards to a mental health services partner should the data show that you may need such services.

Providing personalized services

We use the information we collect to customize our services for you, including providing recommendations, personalized questionaires, and customized application qualities.

Developing new services

We use the information we collect in existing services to help us develop new ones. For example, understanding that people lacked methods of connecting with those with similar interests and life stages made us create the social messaging matching option.

Measuring our performance

We use data for analytics and measurement to understand how our services are used. Understanding which features are used more and how they are used will help us optimize our product design.


Communicating with you

We use information we collect to help us interact with you directly. For example, we may send you notifications on when you should go visit your family and friends as well as when we detect that your account has been accessed from a questionable location. We may also inform you about changes and improvements to our services. If you contact us, we can keep a record of your request in order to help solve any issues you may be facing.

Protecting Lifelong, our users and the public

We use information we collect to help improve the safety and reliability of our services. This includes detecting, preventing, and responding to fraud, abuse, security risks, and technical issues that could harm Lifelong, our users, or the public.

You always have control over what information we collect and how it's used

You can always visit the activity controls of our application to chose what information we collect and what permissions are given in terms of the use of that information. If you want to have all the features of our application that come with personalized features without having your information being processed securely by our centralized systems, that's perfectly cool with us. Just visit the dashboard to manage the specific information disclosed by each activity and action you do. You may also duplicate the information in your lifelong account so that you may export it for use outside our application. You can also request data from us that you may also use free of charge to analyze for any percieved issues you may have.


Sharing your information

Many of our services allow you to share information with others. For example, when you chose to utilize our communications feature you can share the activities you do or keep it hidden from others. You can also share basic personality traits determined by our A.I. tests, though by making this information accessible, it becomes accessible to anybody in your friends circle. We also do not share your personal information with the companies, organizations and any individuals outside of lifelong that we work with unless we recieve your consent to do so. It should be noted that the laws of certain governments require us to share certain types of data. You will, however, be informed of these requirements should you request it.
