Providing personalized services

We use the information we collect to customize our services for you, including providing recommendations, personalized questionaires, and customized application qualities.

We use the information we collect in existing services to help us develop new ones. For example, understanding that people lacked methods of connecting with those with similar interests and life stages made us create the social messaging matching option.

We use data for analytics and measurement to understand how our services are used. Understanding which features are used more and how they are used will help us optimize our product design.

We use information we collect to help improve the safety and reliability of our services. This includes detecting, preventing, and responding to fraud, abuse, security risks, and technical issues that could harm Lifelong, our users, or the public.
