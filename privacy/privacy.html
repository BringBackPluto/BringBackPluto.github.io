Privacy

Lifelong Privacy Policy

By using our application, you've put faith in us to keep your personal information safe. We acknowledge that this is a great responsibility and we strive to improve every day. You always have control over what information you share and what we are allowed to do with it.

The types of information we collect as you use our application

The advantages of our application are best demonstrated when we can optimize our services depending on the preferences of our users. This is achieved through the collection of various types of information, ranging from superficial data such as the language you speak and your age, to more personalized data such as which region you reside, your personality traits or what activities you might find interesting.


The types of activity we may collect include(at your discretion, as always):

  • Your location data
  • Activity preferences
  • Interactions with content
  • Answers to surveys and questionnaires
  • Your interactions with family and friends
  • The degrees of success of recommendations
  • Activity on third-party sites and apps that share our services


Maintaining and improving our services

Your information is also used to ensure our application is working as intended, such as tracking inconsistencies in our data analysis or troubleshooting issues that you report to us. Providing personalized services

What is done with all this data you may ask? Well, the most immediate result is to customize what our application brings you. For example, we will provide you with the information in regards to a mental health services partner should the data show that you may need such services.

We use the information we collect in existing services to help us develop new ones. For example, understanding that people lacked methods of connecting with those with similar interests and life stages made us create the social messaging matching option.
